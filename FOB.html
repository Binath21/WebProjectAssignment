Faculty of Business











The NSBM Faculty of Business is an excellent choice for any student interested in pursuing a career in the field of business.The Faculty develops students with a business mindset and shapesthem into future business leaders. The Faculty of Business's enthusiasm for quality and perfection in its teaching, learning, research, and networking with the business community is the driving force for reaching this aim. Business Management, Human Resource Management, Banking and Finance, Accounting, Business Communications, Industrial Management, and Project Management are among the many specializations offered in collaboration with world-renowned universities, including University College Dublin in Ireland, the University of Victoria in Australia,
and the University of Plymouth in the United Kingdom, as well as NSBM-owned degrees approved by the University Grants Commission. Our graduates will be able to get an international degree while living and working in Sri Lanka as a result of this program. Each department in the Faculty of Business is committed to its students and strives to offer them the greatest educational experience possible. So, if you want to have a good career in business, come to the NSBM Faculty of Business!
