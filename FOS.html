Faculty of Science
















Faculty of Scinece! The Faculty of Science at NSBM is an excellent choice for undergraduates interested in pursuing a career in science. The Faculty now provides two four-year degree programs accredited by the University Grant Commission: Biomedical Science and Public Health and Nutrition, as well as a Nursing degree program in conjunction with the world-renowned University of Plymouth. These programs give science instruction in your chosen area. Science graduates will be a source of health science-related skilled professionals and research scientists with practical skills; multi-faceted team members with planning, project, and business abilities including cost, safety, dependability, and time management; and lifelong learners. Students accepted into the faculty have the opportunity to learn from a highly skilled and competent lecture panel. You have access to cutting-edge laboratories with cutting-edge instrumentation, as well as a variety of student support services to assist you with your specific requirements. You will be more proficient in your subject areas as a result of the strong foundation provided since you will have gained practical skills, rational thinking, analytical, and clinical abilities. With the four years of exceptional experience, you'll obtain at NSBM, you'll be able to confidently accomplish your ambitions as a fully-fledged professional.
