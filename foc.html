Faculty of Computing















The Faculty of Computing (FOC) provides world-class education and training in Computing and Information Technology,both at undergraduate as well as postgraduate levels. FOC provides degree programs in a variety of fields that have been authorized by the University Grants Commission. It has also formed partnerships with top-ranked institutions throughout the world, including University College Dublin in Ireland, the University of Plymouth in the United Kingdom, and Victoria University in Australia, to supply undergraduates with internationally recognized degrees. Our unique teaching techniques, together with the most up-to-date state-of-the-art technology, provide the ideal environment for our students to accomplish their best and achieve their objectives with ease.
