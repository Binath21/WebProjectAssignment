How we handle the covid in campus.


  • Everyone's body temperature is measured before entering the university.
  • If the body temperature rises above 37.5, the person will not be admitted to the university.
  • If a corona patient is found at the university, they will be referred for immediate treatment.
  • Once a week the whole university is disinfected.

Guidline for studenr.


  • Everyone should wear mask.
  • Everyone must wash their hands before entering university.
  • In the lecture hall, each student must open a chair and sit down.
  • It is forbidden to remove the mask in the university.
